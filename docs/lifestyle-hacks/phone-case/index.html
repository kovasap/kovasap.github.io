    3-in-1 Phone Case

    This system for a wallet, keys, and phone in one package has been serving me pretty well. It doesn’t add much noticeable bulk to my phone at all, and even makes it a bit easier to hold because my fingers can grab the pouch instead of the other side of the phone.

    I used a cheap, thin rubber phone case with the Sinjimoru Card Holder for this system.
