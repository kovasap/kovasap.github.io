    Brain Juice

    I’m not sure exactly how, but I feel that my brain behaves similarly to my muscles in that it can tire on both short and long timescales when used. My mind feels tired after making lots of new connections (learning new things) and especially when critically re-validating my existing connections. It also feels tired when doing the same thing many times, even if that thing is a simple expression of a deeply ingrained skill.
