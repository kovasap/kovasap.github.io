    “Intelligence” #

    Informed by:

    • WASL/SAT/GRE tests I’ve taken
    • https://www.wnycstudios.org/story/g-miseducation-larry-p

    In my opinion, intelligence as a concept is a harmful oversimplification. My thoughts on several contexts in which the word is used are described here:

    Ability to Learn #

    Tests judging intelligence, or general mental ability, are used to decide whether students at all levels are capable of participating in certain educational activities. These activities include taking certain courses and being admitted into certain programs or schools.

    Social Perception #

    People use intelligence to describe each other in a general way.
