Great Talks

After each talk are listed my major takeaways from it.

Software

  • Simple made Easy by Rich Hickey
    • Complex = entangled = difficult to reason about
    • To make systems more understandable, need to reduce them to small, independant pieces (like pure functions with no side effects)
  • Thinking Above the Code by Leslie Lamport
    • It is easy to trick yourself that you are thinking rationally when you do not share your thoughts by writing them down or speaking them out loud. If you are serious about solving a problem, write down your thought process!

Game Design

  • 20 Lessons from 20 Years of MTG by Mark Rosewater
    • Creativity comes from constraining your problem space
