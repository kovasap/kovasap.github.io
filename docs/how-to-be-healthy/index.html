    How to Be Healthy

    What should I eat?

    Mostly vegan, with fish 2-3 times per week1.

    Things to avoid

    • Refined sugars
      • Danger of Fast Carbs
    • Red meat

    When should I eat?

    Probably mostly earlier in the day23, and within a 8-12 hour window34.

    • Meal Timing And Frequency Effects on Cardiovascular Disease
      • Eating meals earlier in the day might be healthier
      • Eating meals on a regular schedule is likely healthier

    Should I fast for prolonged periods?

    Probably1.
